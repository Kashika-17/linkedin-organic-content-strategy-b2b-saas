Point-of-sale interactions can successfully convert anonymous shoppers into known customers.

### 4. Compliance drives innovation

Working within regulatory constraints encouraged more creative customer acquisition strategies rather than limiting growth opportunities.

---

### 5. Marketing infrastructure creates sustainable growth

Customer data infrastructure produces long-term business value that extends well beyond individual campaigns.

Brands investing in customer ownership create a durable competitive advantage.
