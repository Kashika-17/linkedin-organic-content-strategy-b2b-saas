# Dave Gerhardt

## Newsletter 1

### Title
Timeless B2B Marketing Lessons

### Published
June 23, 2026

### Author
Dave Gerhardt

### Source
Exit Five Newsletter

### Why Selected

This newsletter explores how exceptional writing, storytelling, and customer research create competitive advantages in an AI-driven marketing environment. The lessons directly apply to LinkedIn organic content because successful LinkedIn posts rely on authentic storytelling, audience understanding, and differentiated communication rather than generic AI-generated copy.

---

### Summary

Dave reflects on a conversation with copywriter Eddie Shleyner, arguing that while AI has dramatically improved research efficiency, human creativity remains the defining factor behind memorable marketing.

Using an experiment comparing AI-generated writing with human storytelling, the newsletter demonstrates that effective copy evokes emotion through observation, dialogue, and context rather than explicitly describing feelings.

Dave also explains that AI should primarily support research and preparation, allowing marketers to dedicate more time to strategic thinking and creative execution.

---

### Key Research Insights

#### Great writing shows instead of tells

Strong marketing copy allows readers to experience emotions naturally rather than explicitly describing them.

For LinkedIn, authentic stories outperform overly promotional messaging.

---

#### Great content begins with research

Customer interviews, reviews, forums, and conversations provide the language that creates persuasive marketing.

AI accelerates research but should not replace human interpretation.

---

#### AI should support creativity

Dave recommends using AI for:

- Research
- Summarization
- Brainstorming
- Organization

Human marketers remain responsible for originality, positioning, storytelling, and judgment.

---

#### Authentic storytelling creates differentiation

As AI-generated content becomes increasingly common, marketers who share personal experiences, observations, and unique perspectives become more memorable.

---

### Why It Matters

This newsletter explains why successful LinkedIn content is driven by thoughtful research, authentic storytelling, and human creativity rather than automation alone.

These principles support sustainable thought leadership for B2B SaaS marketers.

---

### Personal Research Notes

One recurring theme across Dave's content is that AI improves efficiency but cannot replace strategic thinking. Successful LinkedIn content should combine customer research with authentic experiences to create content that feels credible, differentiated, and genuinely useful.

# Dave Gerhardt

## Newsletter 2

### Title

How to Make Your B2B Influencer Marketing Work

### Published

June 16, 2026

### Author

Dave Gerhardt

### Source

Exit Five Newsletter

### Why Selected

This newsletter explains how influencer marketing functions as a brand-building strategy rather than a short-term demand generation tactic. Many of the principles discussed—including creator partnerships, trust, content repurposing, and audience credibility—translate directly into effective LinkedIn organic content strategies.

---

### Summary

Dave challenges the assumption that influencer marketing is only suitable for enterprise companies with large budgets.

Drawing insights from a conversation with Ishveen Jolly (CEO of OpenSponsorship), he argues that successful creator programs depend on narrative alignment, audience trust, and long-term partnerships rather than celebrity status or follower counts.

Instead of treating influencer campaigns as isolated promotional activities, companies should integrate creators into broader brand-building and content distribution strategies.

---

### Key Research Insights

#### Narrative matters more than reach

Successful creator partnerships depend on story alignment rather than audience size.

Brands should collaborate with individuals whose expertise naturally supports the company's positioning.

---

#### Influencer marketing is a brand investment

Creator content should be evaluated using metrics such as:

- Brand awareness
- Reach
- Engagement
- Trust
- Community growth

rather than immediate lead generation.

---

#### Repurpose creator content

Dave emphasizes using creator-generated content across:

- LinkedIn
- Newsletters
- Podcasts
- Events
- Sales enablement
- Case studies

One collaboration should generate multiple content assets.

---

#### Give creators creative freedom

Authentic creator content consistently outperforms heavily scripted promotional campaigns.

Companies should provide objectives while allowing creators to communicate in their own voice.

---

#### Commit for the long term

Small, low-commitment experiments rarely produce meaningful brand impact.

Successful creator strategies require consistent investment, patience, and long-term relationship building.

---

### Why It Matters

The newsletter demonstrates that LinkedIn organic growth is strengthened through trusted voices, authentic partnerships, and educational content rather than purely promotional campaigns.

These principles support long-term brand authority within B2B SaaS markets.

---

### Personal Research Notes

This newsletter reinforces Dave's broader marketing philosophy that trust compounds over time. Creator partnerships, authentic storytelling, and educational content consistently outperform transactional marketing because they strengthen relationships before attempting to generate demand.
