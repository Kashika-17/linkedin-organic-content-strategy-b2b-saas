# Video 1

## Building an Executive Brand on LinkedIn

**Source**
Closing Time Podcast

### Core Message

LinkedIn should be treated as a long-term reputation engine rather than a short-term lead generation channel. Executives who consistently publish valuable content with a clear point of view build authority, trust, and influence that compounds over time.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Start with a Clear Point of View

The fastest way to stand out on LinkedIn is not by posting more frequently but by developing a unique perspective.

Strong opinions make content memorable and differentiate creators from generic business advice.

---

### 2. Build a Strategy Before Posting

Every post should support a business objective.

Before publishing, executives should define:

- Who they want to influence
- What expertise they want to be known for
- How LinkedIn supports business growth

Random posting eventually leads to inconsistent messaging and weak results.

---

### 3. Commit to the Long Game

Reputation is built through consistency.

One viral post does not establish authority.

Publishing valuable content over months and years creates lasting trust.

---

### LinkedIn Video Best Practices

Based on Devin's research of hundreds of LinkedIn videos, high-performing videos typically include:

- 60–90 second duration
- Strong opening hook
- Mobile-first format
- Fast pacing
- Educational value
- Native LinkedIn uploads

He recommends keeping videos under two minutes whenever possible.

---

### Three Executive Content Formats

#### Winning Playbooks

Share the exact process behind successful results.

Instead of saying something worked, explain how it worked.

Use:

- Frameworks
- Screenshots
- Metrics
- Step-by-step breakdowns

---

#### Industry Commentary

Comment on important news rather than simply reporting it.

Explain why the change matters and how it affects the market.

---

#### Vulnerable Lessons

Executives build trust by discussing failures, mistakes, and lessons learned.

Stories about missed opportunities or difficult experiences often resonate more than polished success stories.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Develop a clear point of view.
- Build a content strategy before publishing.
- Prioritize consistency over virality.
- Share practical frameworks with proof.
- Use LinkedIn videos to build trust, not simply generate views.
