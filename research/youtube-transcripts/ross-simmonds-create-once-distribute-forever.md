# YouTube Video 1 — Create Once, Distribute Forever

**Speaker:** Ross Simmonds

**Event:** Exit Five Drive 2024

**Theme:**
Content Distribution | Organic B2B Growth | Content Repurposing | Media Strategy | AI-Assisted Marketing

---

## Executive Summary

In this keynote, Ross Simmonds challenges one of the biggest misconceptions in B2B marketing—that publishing content alone creates growth.

Instead, he argues that modern B2B companies should operate like media organizations where distribution is treated as a core business function rather than an afterthought. Rather than continuously producing new assets, organizations should maximize the value of every piece of content through systematic repurposing, channel expansion, and continuous optimization.

Ross introduces practical frameworks for building repeatable distribution systems that increase organic reach, search visibility, and long-term authority across multiple digital channels.

---

## Key Learnings

### 1. Create once, distribute forever

Ross introduces his core philosophy:

> Create once. Distribute forever.

Instead of treating content as a one-time campaign, marketers should continuously repurpose and redistribute existing assets across multiple platforms to maximize long-term ROI.

---

### 2. Every content asset should deliver one of the Four E's

Ross recommends evaluating content against four characteristics:

- Educational
- Engaging
- Entertaining
- Empowering

If content fails to achieve at least one of these outcomes, it is unlikely to generate meaningful audience engagement.

---

### 3. Distribution follows a four-stage growth framework

Ross outlines a repeatable process for sustainable organic growth:

- Research audience problems
- Create valuable content
- Distribute strategically
- Optimize continuously

Distribution is positioned as an equally important discipline alongside content creation.

---

### 4. Distribution should be channel-native

Rather than posting identical content everywhere, Ross recommends adapting assets for each platform.

Examples include:

- Podcasts into short-form video
- Blog posts into LinkedIn content
- Whitepapers into visual social assets
- Research into newsletter content

Each channel requires its own presentation while maintaining consistent messaging.

---

### 5. AI should enhance—not replace—marketing

Ross positions AI as an execution accelerator.

AI can support:

- Research
- Repurposing
- Asset creation
- Workflow automation

Strategic thinking, editorial judgment, storytelling, and audience understanding remain human responsibilities.

---

## Why This Video Matters

This presentation captures Ross Simmonds' complete philosophy around modern B2B content marketing.

Rather than publishing more content, organizations should invest in repeatable distribution systems, continuous optimization, and platform-specific content strategies that compound organic reach over time.

The keynote reinforces that distribution—not creation alone—is becoming the primary competitive advantage in the AI era.
