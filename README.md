# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Research Project

This repository documents a structured research project focused on LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS companies.

The objective is to identify industry-leading practitioners, analyze their content strategies, document recurring frameworks, and build a high-quality research repository that can later be transformed into practical playbooks and growth strategies.

---

## Objectives

- Identify top LinkedIn creators in B2B SaaS
- Research content frameworks and growth strategies
- Analyze engagement patterns
- Collect YouTube transcripts and LinkedIn posts
- Organize findings into a structured knowledge base
- Build reusable playbooks for future implementation
